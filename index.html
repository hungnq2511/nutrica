PRIVACY POLICY

Nutrica – AI Food Tracker & AI Food Scan

Last Updated: 23 February 2026

Nutrica (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, process, and safeguard your information when you use the Nutrica mobile application (the “App”).

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Height, weight, target weight
  • Date of birth
  • Gender
  • Daily activity level
  • Food intake data (meals, calories, macronutrients)
  • Water intake records
  • Step count data (if enabled)
  • Food images for AI analysis
  • Email address (if linking account)
  • Account credentials (if registered)

Providing health and nutrition data is voluntary and used only to deliver core functionality.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device model and operating system
  • App version
  • Crash logs and diagnostics
  • Anonymous usage analytics

We do not collect precise location data unless explicitly granted by the user.

2. Health and Nutrition Data Usage

Nutrition and lifestyle data is used solely to:

  • Calculate calorie needs
  • Track progress
  • Provide AI-based food recognition
  • Generate personalized recommendations
  • Enable gamification and reward systems

Health-related data is never sold and is not shared for advertising purposes.

3. AI Food Scan & Third-Party AI Processing

When you use the AI Food Scan feature:

  • The selected food image is securely transmitted via encrypted HTTPS connection.
  • The image is sent to our AI processing provider: OpenAI, L.L.C..
  • The purpose of transmission is strictly to analyze food items and estimate calorie and nutrition values.

3.1 What Data Is Sent

  • The food image you upload
  • Optional contextual prompt data required for analysis

No account passwords, health history, or unrelated personal data are sent to the AI provider.

3.2 User Consent

Before using AI Food Scan for the first time, users are informed that their selected food image will be processed by a third-party AI service and must provide explicit consent.

Users may choose not to use the AI Food Scan feature at any time.

3.3 Data Storage & Retention

  • Images are processed for analysis purposes only.
  • Images are not used for advertising or marketing profiling.
  • We do not sell or license image data.
  • Food scan results are stored in the user’s account for tracking history unless deleted.

OpenAI processes data in accordance with its own privacy and security standards and does not use API-submitted data for advertising purposes.

4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party providers for:

  • Cloud database storage
  • Authentication services
  • Analytics and crash reporting
  • AI processing services (OpenAI, L.L.C.)

These providers process data solely on our behalf and under contractual obligations requiring appropriate data protection.

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

5. Data Storage & Access

  • User account and nutrition data are stored in secure cloud infrastructure.
  • Data is protected via encrypted transmission (HTTPS).
  • Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Users may request account deletion at any time.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Withdraw consent for AI processing
  • Request data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

8. Account Deletion

Users can request permanent account deletion by:

  • Using the in-app “Delete Account” feature, or
  • Contacting support via email.

Upon deletion:

  • Personal data will be permanently deleted within a reasonable timeframe.
  • Some information may be retained if legally required.

9. Data Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure authentication systems
  • Restricted internal access controls

However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

10. Children’s Privacy

Nutrica is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. International Users

Your information may be processed in countries outside your residence. We implement safeguards to ensure lawful and secure data transfers.
