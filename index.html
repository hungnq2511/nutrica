PRIVACY POLICY

Nutrica – AI Food Tracker & AI Food Scan

Last Updated: 20 February 2026

Nutrica (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you use the Nutrica mobile application (the “App”).

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When using Nutrica, you may provide:

  • Height, weight, target weight
  • Date of birth
  • Gender
  • Daily activity level
  • Food intake data (meals, calories, macronutrients)
  • Water intake records
  • Step count data (if enabled)
  • Food images for AI analysis
  • Email address (if linking account)
  • Account credentials (if registered)

Providing health and nutrition data is voluntary and used only to deliver core functionality.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Device model and operating system
  • App version
  • Crash logs and diagnostics
  • Anonymous usage analytics

We do not collect precise location data unless explicitly granted by the user.

2. Health and Nutrition Data

Nutrica collects nutrition and lifestyle data solely to:

  • Calculate calorie needs
  • Track progress
  • Provide AI-based food recognition
  • Generate personalized recommendations
  • Enable gamification and reward systems

Health-related data is not shared with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. AI Food Scan & Image Processing

  • Images are processed to identify food items and estimate nutrition values.
  • Images may be temporarily stored for processing purposes.
  • Users may delete food history at any time within the App.

We do not use food images for advertising profiling.

4. Guest Account

  • Guest data is stored locally on the device.
  • Deleting the App may permanently delete guest data.
  • Users may link an email account to enable cloud backup and synchronization.

5. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the App
  • Personalize nutrition calculations
  • Track user progress
  • Provide rewards and gamification features
  • Improve AI accuracy
  • Respond to support requests
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse

We do not sell personal data.

6. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services for:

  • Cloud database storage
  • Authentication services
  • Analytics and crash reporting
  • AI processing services

These providers process data only on our behalf and in compliance with applicable privacy laws. Nutrica does not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Users may request account deletion at any time.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Withdraw consent
  • Request data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

9. Account Deletion

Users can request permanent account deletion by:

  • Using the in-app “Delete Account” feature, or
  • Contacting support via email.

Upon deletion request:

  • Personal data will be permanently deleted within a reasonable timeframe.
  • Some data may be retained if legally required.

10. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure authentication systems
  • Restricted internal access

However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

11. Children’s Privacy

Nutrica is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such data collection, we will delete it promptly.

12. International Users

Your information may be stored and processed in countries outside your residence. We take appropriate safeguards to ensure lawful data transfer.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted within the App and on our website with an updated revision date.
